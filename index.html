The game of mao

The Game of Mao is a wonderfully horrid card game that's sure to destroy your family ties faster than a tornado in a trailer park! With its top-secret rules that can't be shared, you'll spend hours arguing with your loved ones over what's allowed and what's not. But don't worry, that's all part of the fun!

Sound fun? Well would you like to learn to play? You are just in luck.

Because of the few rules that a Mao master is allowed to share is that we cannot share the rules!
